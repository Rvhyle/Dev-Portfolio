About Me

Hello, My name is Ryle and I'm a self-taught front end developer from Queens, New York. Growing up I was obsessed with video games, mostly shooters and because of this obsession I became curious on how these video games were made and what made them so great. After a little digging, I discovered code. Front end development wasn't my first choice, before I began front end I was testing the waters with different languages and different ways to create things with code. But, everything changed when the pandemic hit. Lockdown forced us to stay home and as a result, I decided to dedicate the time to learn a new skill and commit to it, that's when I decided to try and learn web development. I started doing YouTube tutorials, buying Udemy courses , and building my own mini projects. As time went on, I fell in love with the process and I finally knew what I wanted to do. Till this day, I'm still learning and everyday is a new opportunity for me to become a better developer.
